Avocado souse (perfect for quinoa)🥑

  1. Avocado 2pc.
  2. Garlic (fresh) as much as you like
  3. Onion (fresh) as much as you like
  4. Parsley (fresh) as much as you like
  5. Lemon juice 0.5 lemon or a little bit less
  6. Black cumin 0.5 tea spoon
  7. Sour cream 2 tea spoons
  8. Salt and Pepper

Mix everything in blender and enjoy

Tabbouleh salad

Let bulgur in cold water for an hour or unless it becomes soft. Meanwhile cut fresh dill and green onion (preferably leak onion). Add the soft bulgur and let's prepare sauce. Take two soup spoons of tomato paste, add sault. black pepper, half of a tea spoon sugar, and olive oil and mix. Add the sauce to the bulgur and mix everything together. You can cut fresh tomatoes inside and enjoy.

Dish with aubergines, perfect with rice

Fry aubergines on olive oil, fry sweet pepper Fry onion, add to it peeled and cutted into small pieces tomatoes, add salt and pepper. After a couple of minutes add the fried aubergines and pepper. Take out from from stove. Add fresh koriandr. You can add fresh garlic to it. Bon appetite! I usually prefer to peel the aubergines and cut them in slices and fry for both sides. The sweet pepper I cut in small squares. I also like to fry everything in olive oil, usually with olive oil it is tastier, but it is up to you how to cut and fry.
